How we started.

Daho Foundation is a charitable organization registered in Kenya. Reg No. R49752 Since 2017. The Organization endeavours to serve the most underprevileged members of the community in both urban informal settlements in Kenya.

Through various projects in water, health, Sanitation, Hygiene, Empowerment & Education Since its inception the organization in partnership, collaboration with various stakeholders have worked to ease the burden of communities towards attainment of the SDGs.
